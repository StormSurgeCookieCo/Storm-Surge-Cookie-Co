About Us

Storm Surge Cookie Company was born on the West Coast, where rain has a rhythm and comfort comes warm from the oven. Inspired by our rugged terrain, foggy mornings, and long stretches of driftwood filled shoreline, we bake thick, indulgent cookies meant to be enjoyed when the weather turns grey and stormy. These cookies are for rainy days, salty air and slowing down with coffee in hand while beachcombing, because on the coast, the best moments happen when you lean into the storm.
